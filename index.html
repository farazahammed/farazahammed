Who is Faraz Ahammed CM?

I'm Faraz Ahammed CM — known online as FRZ CM — a student entrepreneur and digital creator from Vazhayur, Kerala. While most students my age are focused purely on academics, I've been busy building platforms, creating educational tools, and growing YouTube channels that serve real audiences.

In 2025, I founded FRZ NOTES, an educational platform providing quality study materials, and FRZ Generations, a suite of 7+ productivity and learning tools for students. I co-founded HF Media Malayalam back in 2021 — now with 74+ videos and 310K+ combined views — and joined as Managing Partner of English Daily Shots in 2025.

I believe entrepreneurship doesn't require an age limit. Every tool I build, every video I create — it's all aimed at making learning more accessible and engaging for students across Kerala and India.
